MergeBlast

Drop, merge, and strategize in this premium take on the classic 2048 puzzle! Dive into a sleek, ad-free experience where every move counts as you aim to combine tiles and reach the ultimate target: 8192.

How to Play

1

Drop & Merge

Drop numbered tiles onto the grid and merge matching numbers (2+2=4, 4+4=8, etc.) to create higher values.

2

Strategize

Strategize each drop to build combos and work toward the legendary 8192 tile.

3

Track Progress

Track your progress and strive to surpass your personal best score in every game.
