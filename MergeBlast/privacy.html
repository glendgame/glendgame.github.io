Privacy Policy

Our commitment to your privacy

Introduction

At GlendGame, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding the MergeBlast game application.

Information Collection

We do not collect any personal information from our users. MergeBlast is designed to function without the need to collect, store, or process any personal data from you.

No Data Collection Practices

MergeBlast:

  • Does not collect personal information
  • Does not track your location
  • Does not access your contacts
  • Does not request access to your photos or media
  • Does not use cookies or similar tracking technologies
  • Does not collect device information
  • Does not include third-party analytics tools

Game Data Storage

Any game progress, scores, or settings are stored locally on your device only. This data remains on your device and is not transmitted to our servers or any third parties.

Children's Privacy

MergeBlast is suitable for users of all ages. Since we do not collect any personal information from any users, including children, there are no special provisions for children's data.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: glendstd@hotmail.com
