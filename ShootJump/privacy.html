Privacy Policy for Shoot Jump

Effective Date: May 25, 2025

1. Introduction

Glendgame ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information when you use our mobile game, Shoot Jump ("the App").

2. Information We Do Not Collect

The App is designed to operate without collecting any personal or non-personal information from users. Specifically:

  • No Personal Data: We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, location data, or any other personally identifiable information.
  • No Usage Data: We do not track your interactions with the App, device information, IP addresses, or browsing history.
  • No Third-Party Analytics: The App does not integrate with analytics services (e.g., Google Analytics) or advertising platforms.

3. Local Data Storage

Shoot Jump may store game progress, settings, and other data locally on your device. This data is not transmitted to us or any third parties and can be deleted by uninstalling the App.

4. Children's Privacy

The App is designed to be family-friendly and does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. Since we do not collect any data, there is no risk of inadvertently gathering information from minors.

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. When we do, we will revise the effective date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
