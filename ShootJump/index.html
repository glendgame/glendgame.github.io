About Shoot Jump

Shoot Jump is an innovative game that combines shooting mechanics with vertical platforming. Designed specifically for iWatch, this game offers a unique gaming experience right on your wrist. Use the recoil from your shots to propel yourself upward and reach new heights!

Core Gameplay: Shoot to Jump

Unique Mechanism

Every shot is a force for upward jumps. Control recoil with precise taps, turning bullet trajectories into your "springboard"!

Ammo Strategy

Limited bullets require flexible shooting rhythm planning. Pick up magazines in the scene to replenish ammo and avoid falling due to "jamming"!

Challenges and Goals

Every round is an ultimate test of reflexes and strategy! When the game ends, the system permanently records your highest jumping height, allowing you to compete with global players to become the "Recoil Jump Master"!
